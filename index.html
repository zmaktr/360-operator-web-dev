ABOUT US

Welcome to 360 Operator

For us, the people are the core of everything and our purpose is to support each and everybody in accomplishing their individual objectives and desires.

With our 360° view of the people’s expectations and individual needs, we provide and develop holistic operator concepts with brands suitable for every phase of life and lifestyle, because the real estate market is developing in a cyclical dynamic manner.

Advanced ways of mobility and networking, as well as demographic change, require more innovative living concepts.

In response to such requirements and developments, 360° living stands for creating a modern and flexible range of services and spaces inside and outside of one’ s own four walls at home, which can be individually adapted to any lifestyle.

Individual, Flexible, Dynamic

Our aim is to develop and operate cyclically dynamic brands that reflect the entire life cycle as far as possible. From childcare facilities to retirement homes, a variety of concepts are offered within the 360 operator world.

Our Management

Rolf Schneider

CEO - 360 Operators

Rolf Schneider is CEO of 360 Operator and is responsible for the entire operational business with the areas of asset management, construction & development, property management, facility management and most of the departments. He is also responsible for the digitization process of all operational units. Anja Müller

COO - 360 Operators

Anja Müller joined 360 Operator GmbH in April 2020 as COO and Managing Director. She leads the strategic brand development and growth of the 360 Operator platform. Over 20 + years of experience in top positions in the international hospitality industry.
